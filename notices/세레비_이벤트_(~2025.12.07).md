## ◆ 획득 방법
![gs볼](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252FAqfxTWxbvY7m2ENNS1E6%252Fgsball.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D174f5496-da22-4850-9984-719a1f84afa0&width=768&dpr=1&quality=100&sign=1f8a66f3&sv=2)  
세레비를 소환하기 위해서는 우선 위 레시피를 따라 **gs볼**을 제작하고,

![세레비제단](https://legendary-monuments.gitbook.io/legendary-monuments-wiki/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3671020307-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FRGT15Y5jJBgESLNQuMWJ%252Fuploads%252F72dp57tCWEX9H6HV6Lvz%252Filexshrine.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Da7acc6a4-c181-4d28-88c1-02a8ae6ad637&width=768&dpr=1&quality=100&sign=ccbd83a0&sv=2)  
위와 같은 **숲의 제단**을 찾아서 gs볼을 사용하면 됩니다.  
숲의 제단은 숲 계열 지형에서 발견됩니다.
